About Us

The Information Technology Institute (ITI) is a leading Egyptian institution offering advanced training and capacity-building programs in the fields of information and communication technology (ICT). Established in 1993 under the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT), ITI aims to equip young professionals and university graduates with the skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.
